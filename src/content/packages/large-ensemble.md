For couples who want a fuller, more cinematic live sound. The Large Ensemble is Allegra's
fullest formation: 4 violins, 2 violas, 2 cellos, double bass, and piano — 10 musicians by
default — for a richer, more orchestral arrangement. Piano is part of the default lineup,
and additional musicians or a customised instrumentation can be arranged on request.
