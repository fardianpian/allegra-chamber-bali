## Competitive Implications for Allegra Chamber Bali

**Where they're strong vs. us**: Authentic, frequent real-wedding video proof on a channel (Instagram) where wedding-planner referrals and couples are already browsing.

**Where we're strong vs. them**: Allegra has (or can build) an actual SEO-indexed website — meaning Allegra can capture the entire "wedding string quartet Bali" / "wedding pianist Bali" Google search demand that Sundaram cannot access at all. Allegra can also publish pricing-adjacent trust signals (FAQ, packages, formations, reviews/testimonials) that Sundaram has none of.

**Threats**: If Sundaram is the music act a wedding planner already has a relationship with and routinely recommends, word-of-mouth/referral channel bookings can bypass search entirely — Allegra's planner-facing `/for-planners` page and outreach matters more against this competitor than SEO does.
