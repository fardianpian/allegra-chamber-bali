The Formations

Five Ways to Hear Allegra

Solo

A single violin, cello, or wedding pianist — the most intimate option for a vow exchange or small ceremony.

Ideal for intimate ceremonies, vow exchanges, cliffside elopements

Duo

Violin and cello, joined by piano as standard — a warmer, fuller sound than solo, with our signature piano blended in from the start.

Ideal for ceremony, cocktail hour · 3 musicians (piano included)

Trio

Violin, viola, cello, and piano — full chamber colour with piano included as standard, for ceremony through cocktail hour.

Ideal for ceremony, cocktail hour, smaller receptions · 4 musicians (piano included)

String Quartet

The complete chamber experience — two violins, viola, cello, and piano — for ceremony through reception. Our signature formation, with piano included as standard.

Ideal for processional & recessional, cocktail hour, reception sets · 5 musicians (piano included)

Large Ensemble

Four violins, two violas, two cellos, double bass, and piano — our fullest formation for a layered, statement sound. 10 musicians by default; scalable further on request.

Ideal for grand ceremony entrances, large receptions & gala dinners · 10 musicians (default)

Good to Know
  • All prices and transactions are in Indonesian Rupiah (IDR).
  • Prices shown above are final for the formation and coverage listed — not estimates, subject only to date availability. Deposit and overtime (see below) are separate and confirmed once your booking details are set.
  • Ceremony and Cocktail Only are priced the same for Solo, Duo, and Trio — a shorter Ceremony set needs the same setup, travel, and precision timing (processional, signing) as a full Cocktail hour, so pricing reflects the booking as a whole, not just minutes played. For String Quartet and Large Ensemble, Cocktail is priced slightly above Ceremony, reflecting the added coordination of a larger ensemble in a mingling / background setting.
  • Piano is included as standard in Duo, Trio, String Quartet, and Large Ensemble — it's Allegra's flagship instrument, not a paid upgrade. Solo is the only formation where you choose a single instrument (violin, cello, or piano).
  • All prices include a digital/electric piano. Amplification for the piano is not included — that falls under sound system, which we don't provide (see below). We do not provide an acoustic piano (upright, baby grand, or grand) either — if you'd like one at your venue, we're happy to recommend a piano rental vendor on request.
  • Allegra is a live-music ensemble only — we do not provide wedding planning or coordination services. Please arrange these with your wedding planner or organizer.
  • Allegra does not provide a sound system / PA package, including amplification for the electric piano — this is arranged separately through your venue or AV vendor. Our string instruments perform acoustically; the electric piano needs a nearby power outlet, and we're happy to share our technical requirements with your sound vendor on request.
  • Large Ensemble's default lineup is 4 violins, 2 violas, 2 cellos, double bass, and piano (10 musicians); exact instrumentation can be customised on request. Additional string musicians beyond the default 10 are billed at a flat Rp 2,500,000 per musician, per session, reflecting custom arrangement — see "Scaling Up" above.
  • Outdoor ceremonies need a shaded, weather-protected space for musicians and equipment — direct sun and rain can damage instruments and electronics.
  • A deposit is required to confirm your booking date, and overtime beyond your booked coverage is available on request subject to musician availability — both are confirmed separately once your booking is set.
  • Repertoire, including your processional or first-dance song, is arranged together with you ahead of the wedding.
