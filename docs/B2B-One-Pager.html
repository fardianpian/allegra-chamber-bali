Partner Reference Sheet

Updated June 2026

Allegra Chamber Bali is a live chamber ensemble — piano and strings — performing ceremonies and receptions for destination weddings across Bali. This sheet covers what you need to vet us as a vendor for your couples: the formations on offer, how we handle logistics, and a sample of the repertoire.

The Formations

Five Ways to Hear Allegra

Solo

A single violin, cello, or wedding pianist — the most intimate option for a vow exchange or small ceremony.

Ideal for intimate ceremonies, vow exchanges, cliffside elopements

Duo

Two musicians, typically violin and cello, for a warmer, fuller sound than solo.

Ideal for ceremony, cocktail hour

Trio

Violin, viola, and cello — full chamber colour for ceremony through cocktail hour.

Ideal for ceremony, cocktail hour, smaller receptions

String Quartet

The complete chamber experience — two violins, viola, and cello — for ceremony through reception. Our signature formation.

Ideal for processional & recessional, cocktail hour, reception sets

Large Ensemble

String quartet plus piano and double bass (6 musicians) for a layered, statement sound. Piano is included in this default lineup.

Ideal for grand ceremony entrances, large receptions & gala dinners

Piano + Duo / Trio / Quartet is also available on request, for a fuller, more contemporary blend.

Logistics

Working With Us

  • Tech rider & stage plot on request Stage plot, input list, and power requirements are available for every formation — message us and we'll send them over.
  • One point of contact, 24-hour response No chasing different people for the same answer — confirmations, changes, and day-of questions all go through the same contact.
  • Setup finished before guests arrive Our musicians arrive ahead of schedule and follow a run-of-show confirmed with you in advance, so there's nothing left to manage on site.
  • Outdoor & non-standard venues are routine Cliffside, beach, and garden ceremonies are part of our regular calendar across Bali. Setup stays simple — shade for the musicians and a flat, stable surface for the instruments. Tell us the venue and we'll confirm what's needed.
  • Repertoire arranged to the couple's own songs Conservatory-trained musicians performing without backing tracks — most requests, classical, film, or pop, can be arranged for strings, piano, or both.
