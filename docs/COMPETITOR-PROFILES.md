## 3 Attack Angles

### Attack Angle 1 — "Chamber, not a band"

**Target competitor:** Silaen Music, LOVA Entertainment, Bali Entertainment Agency

Every pop/vocal band in this market uses the same messaging ("live music," "unforgettable
atmosphere," "your favourite songs"). None positions the product as classical chamber — because
none of them are.

**Copy direction:**
> "We don't do vocals or drums. Just piano and strings, precisely arranged for each moment of
> your ceremony. Solo piano for the procession. Strings for the vow exchange. A six-piece
> chamber ensemble for the first dance — if you want it."

**Where to use:** Home hero subhead, /packages intro, any head-to-head comparison context,
social media (formation Reels especially), cold email B2B angle.

---

### Attack Angle 2 — "Bali-based, no fly-in"

**Target competitor:** DIVO Music (Jakarta), any Jakarta-based act

DIVO's 50-piece orchestra is impressive but requires full logistics coordination from Jakarta
(travel, accommodation, PA system transport, different time zones for communication).

**Copy direction:**
> "Based in Bali. We rehearse here, perform here, and know the clifftop stages, outdoor
> acoustics, and 6 PM wind conditions at Uluwatu venues — not from a briefing document, but from
> experience."

**Where to use:** /for-planners page (planners care deeply about day-of logistics risk),
B2B cold email, Bridestory listing, any FAQ about logistics.

---

### Attack Angle 3 — "Your song arranged, not covered"

**Target competitor:** All cover bands (Silaen, LOVA, DIVO)

Every competitor's default is "150+ popular cover songs" or "we can play your favourite."
Allegra's differentiator is that the couple's chosen song is **arranged for classical
instrumentation** — not just played in the original key by a vocalist.

**Copy direction:**
> "The difference isn't the song. It's hearing your song the way it was never played before —
> arranged for piano, violin, and cello. That's what couples remember when they watch it back."

**Where to use:** Home brand essence section (once testimonials unblock it), social Reel
captions, Journal article angle ("What does 'custom arrangement' actually mean?"), pitch
angle for editorial PR.
