> Allegra Chamber Bali performs live chamber music for weddings across Bali — ceremony, cocktail hour, and reception. Five formations available: Solo (violin, cello, or piano), Duo, Trio, String Quartet, and Large Ensemble (string quartet + piano + double bass, six musicians).
>
> Piano is a flagship instrument in every formation, not an add-on. Every arrangement is built around the couple's own songs — processional, first dance, vow exchange.
>
> Conservatory-trained musicians. Real-time performance that adapts to ceremony pacing. Tech rider and stage plot available on request.
>
> Based in Bali, Indonesia. Available across all major venue areas: Uluwatu (Alila Villas Uluwatu, Bvlgari Resort Bali), Ubud, Seminyak, Canggu, Nusa Dua, Sanur.
>
> Bilingual team (English & Indonesian). Inquire via the website — we respond within 24 hours.

Five formations, custom song arrangements, conservatory-trained musicians. English & Indonesian service.
